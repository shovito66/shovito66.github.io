About Me

Hi, I'm Shovito Barua Soumma from Bangladesh currently working as a Full-Time Software Engineer in IQVIA (North Carolina based Clinical Research Industry). Before that, I worked as an Adjunct Lecturer in East Delta University. I received my Bachelors (B.Sc.) in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) from Bangladesh University of Engineering & Technology (BUET) in May 2022. In the future, I aspire to be a competent researcher in the interdisciplinary field of human-computer interaction, privacy/security and applied machine learning and curious to develop systems that can interact with humans to solve daily life complications.

I did my undergraduate thesis in the area of Data mining and Information Systems under the supervision of Dr. Abu Syed Md. Latiful Haque. I have also collaborated with Dr. Md Shohrab Hossain, Dr. A.K.M Ashikur Rahman and Dr. A B M Alim Al Islam to explore different domains of CS.

Apart from my academic activities, I enjoy most of my time being outdoors. and it's in my bucket list to to travel to 10 countries before I turn 40. I like to hangout with my friends and family in my off-time and love to taste new foods whenever I can.
